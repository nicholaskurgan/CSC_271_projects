Common Issues

Here are some common issues that people have with their 3d printers

Having extrision issues, check the condition of your extruder gear and heater block. if the gears are damaged or misaligned remedy them. If the heater core is blocked, either clear or replace it.

Having axis issues, these are harder to nail down, the most likely issues are your linear rails need to be lubricated and cleaned. Improper slicer settings can affect this too.

Having nozzle issues. It its best to replace the nozzle as they are usually cheap, if you have an expensive or special nozzle cleaning it out will be a good solution

Having belt issues. Retension the belts and if they are still beeing problemating the may need to be realigned or replaced

Having adhesion issues. add elmers glue stuck to your bed as a first layer. if the print bed is old or worn out consider replacing it
