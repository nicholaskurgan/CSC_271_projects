Welcome to the Printbin 1.0 website! Here is a place for nerds to talk about and discuss 3d printing.
We have discussions, pictures, even community troubleshooting guides for you to use. Best of all this
is a small site meaning no reddit moderators to stop the fun, we have character. And you should jump in
and add to that character and get stuck in. Make a profile and start posting, everyone is welcome.
